Our Story

Maspejo Company Limited was founded with a vision to revolutionize the construction industry through innovation, quality, and sustainability. With decades of combined experience in construction, architectural design, project management, and renovation, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Our journey is marked by a commitment to excellence and a passion for creating spaces that inspire and endure.

  • Design and Tendering

What We Do

Maspejo Company Limited is composed of a team of professionals including Architects, Quantity Surveyors, Civil Engineers, and Builders, supported by complementary professionals from the built environment. We offer a wide range of services including:

Building & Civil Engineering Projects:

Developing and executing infrastructure projects such as roads, bridges, and utilities that are crucial to community development. Also, we Construct residential, commercial, and industrial buildings that meet the highest standards of quality and functionality.

Feasibility/Appraisal Studies:

Conducting thorough studies to assess the viability and potential of projects

Employer’s Representative for Design-Build Projects:

Representing clients in design-build or Build Operate and Transfer (BOT) projects to ensure seamless execution.

Design-Build Contractors:

Offering integrated design-build solutions that streamline project delivery and enhance efficiency.
